Programs & Services

A comprehensive, holistic approach to recovery — addressing the physical, emotional, and spiritual needs of every resident.

Our Approach

Whole-Person Recovery

Our organization offers a comprehensive suite of services designed to support individuals on their journey to sobriety, foster personal growth, and encourage active community involvement. These services are grounded in a holistic approach that addresses the physical, emotional, and spiritual needs of each participant.

Pillar One

Structured Support for Sobriety

Our program provides a robust framework for individuals seeking to achieve and maintain sobriety.

  • Individualized treatment plans tailored to each resident
  • Access to professional counseling and therapy
  • Group and one-on-one therapy sessions
  • Educational workshops on addiction and recovery
  • Relapse prevention tools and ongoing accountability

Pillar Two

Personal Growth & Development

We place a strong emphasis on personal growth, offering resources and opportunities for individuals to develop their full potential.

  • Life skills training for independent living
  • Vocational and educational support
  • Personal development workshops
  • Goal-setting with mentors and coaches
  • Ongoing encouragement and accountability

Pillar Three

Community Involvement & Integration

Recognizing the importance of community in the recovery process, our program encourages active participation in community service and local events.

  • Community service and volunteer opportunities
  • Partnerships with local organizations and leaders
  • Participation in local cultural events
  • Building positive connections and a support network
  • Opportunities to give back and serve others

Pillar Four

Hawaiian Values & the Spirit of ʻOhana

At the heart of our program is the integration of Hawaiian values and the spirit of ʻohana. These cultural principles provide a strong foundation for healing and growth.

Aloha — Compassion & Love

Our services are delivered with aloha, embodying compassion, empathy, and love. We strive to create an environment where every individual feels valued, respected, and cared for.

Pono — Righteousness & Balance

We encourage participants to live in pono — striving for righteousness and balance in all aspects of their lives, making ethical choices and seeking harmony in relationships.

Lōkahi — Unity & Harmony

We promote a collaborative environment where individuals support one another and work together toward common goals, strengthening bonds within our community.

ʻOhana — Family

The concept of ʻohana extends beyond blood — it embraces the idea of a supportive and nurturing community where belonging and interconnectedness are central.
