Your Impact

How Your Support Makes a Difference

🏠

Maintaining the Facility

Your donations help us provide a secure, substance-free living environment — funding housing maintenance, utilities, and essential supplies.

💬

Supporting Residents

Contributions fund therapy sessions, life skills training, vocational support, and the dedicated staff who provide personalized care.

🌱

Growing Our Impact

With your help, we can expand our services to reach more individuals in need and deepen our community outreach efforts.

🌺

Cultural Activities

Donations fund cultural and recreational activities that reconnect residents with their roots and build healthy relationships.

Other Ways to Give

Beyond Monetary Donations

In-Kind Donations

We welcome donations of supplies and equipment that support our daily operations:

  • Furniture and household items
  • Kitchen appliances and supplies
  • Educational materials
  • Recreational equipment
  • Professional services (legal, medical, vocational)

Volunteer Opportunities

Your time and skills are another valuable way to support Noho Ola Kūpaʻa:

  • Mentorship and tutoring
  • Organizing community events
  • Vocational skills workshops
  • Cultural activities and outreach
  • General facility support

Corporate Sponsorships

Partner With Us

If you represent a business, corporate sponsorships are a powerful way to support our mission while aligning your brand with a meaningful cause in the Hawaiʻi community.
