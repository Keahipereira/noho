Empowering Success Stories

The True Measure of Our Program

We believe that the true measure of our program's success is reflected in the lives of the individuals who have been part of our community. These stories highlight the positive impact of our comprehensive approach to sobriety, personal growth, and community engagement.

Each story is unique, showcasing the diverse backgrounds and challenges our participants have overcome — and the renewed hope, stability, and purpose they have found.

Testimonials

Voices from Our ʻOhana

"When I first joined Noho Ola Kūpaʻa, I was at my lowest point. Struggling with addiction and feeling isolated, I didn't believe there was a way out. The structured support and compassionate care I received here changed everything. The emphasis on Hawaiian values, especially the spirit of ʻohana, made me feel like I was part of a family. I not only achieved sobriety but also rediscovered my passion for helping others. Today, I am a mentor within the program, giving back to the community that saved my life."

— Steve T., 2 years sober

Our Impact

What Transformation Looks Like

Comprehensive Recovery

Our holistic approach addresses all aspects of an individual's well-being — from physical health to emotional stability and spiritual fulfillment.

Cultural Integration

By embracing Hawaiian values and the spirit of ʻohana, we create a nurturing environment where residents feel connected, supported, and rooted.

Community Engagement

Participation in community service helps residents build a sense of purpose and belonging — crucial for lasting recovery and a meaningful life.

The Noho Ola Kūpaʻa ʻOhana

The Noho Ola Kūpaʻa community thrives on mutual support, shared values, and collective growth. Our residents not only work toward their own personal goals but also contribute to the well-being of others, fostering a culture of empathy and solidarity.
