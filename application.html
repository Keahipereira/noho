Application for Residency

Take the First Step

We are excited that you are considering joining our program. Your journey to a brighter future begins here.

How to Apply

What You'll Need

To apply for residency, please have the following information ready. This helps us evaluate your application and provide the best possible support for your journey.

  • Full Legal Name — Your complete legal name as it appears on ID
  • Phone Number — A valid number where we can reach you
  • Email Address — Your primary email for correspondence
  • Employer — Current employer name, if applicable
  • Clean / Sobriety Date — The date you began your sobriety journey

Ready to Begin?

Our online application takes just a few minutes to complete. Click below to access the form in Google Docs.

Once submitted, Lisa will reach out to you within 1–2 business days to discuss next steps.

What to Expect
